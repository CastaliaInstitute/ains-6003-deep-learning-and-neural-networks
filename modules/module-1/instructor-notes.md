# Module 1 Instructor Notes

## Teaching Goal

Students should leave the first module with a stable conceptual map of the field rather than a loose collection of examples.

## Misconceptions to Watch For

- treating all AI systems as machine learning systems
- assuming generative AI represents the whole field
- confusing automation with intelligence in every case
- assuming symbolic AI is obsolete rather than differently suited

## Facilitation Notes

- Ask students to classify examples before you introduce the categories formally.
- Use one symbolic case, one predictive case, and one generative case early.
- Keep returning to the question: what problem structure is the method responding to?

## Grading Cue

In the assignment, prioritize the quality of explanation over whether students choose the exact same category you would. Strong reasoning matters more than rigid labeling.
